Three glass tiers — each frosts the aurora behind it
tier 1 · chrome
.surface-glass

Sidebar, headers, bulk bars. ~0.55 fill · blur(28) saturate(180%).

.surface-card

Device cards, settings panels, list containers. Softer float shadow.

tier 3 · modals
.surface-strong

Dialogs over a dim scrim. Raised to ~0.92 fill so text stays readable on the muddy composite.

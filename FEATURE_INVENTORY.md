# CopyPaste — Merged Feature Inventory

> Consolidated from **20 audit fragments** (8 "MINE" scope audits + 12 external A–I / P1–P3 audits) into a single
> de-duplicated feature inventory. Rows describing the same feature across fragments were merged; where two sources
> disagreed on a feature's status the **more pessimistic** verdict is kept and the disagreement is noted inline as
> `(disputed: …)`. **101 distinct feature rows** are organized under the 12 required categories. Support columns use
> ✅ present / ⚠️ partial / ✗ missing-where-expected / — N/A. Quality ∈ {Complete, Mostly complete, Partial, Stub,
> Broken, Risky, Needs major improvement}. Severity is the worst gap on the row (P0 highest, "—" = no gap).
> No rows were truncated.
